WELCOME TO BUTTERNUT BAKERY! THANKS SO MUCH FOR STOPPING BY

I’m Jenna, the girl behind the counter and the camera. Ever since I can remember, baking has been that one thing that lights me up. There isn’t a day that goes by that I’m not thinking or dreaming about desserts. From cookies to cakes to bars to brownies, it’s my passion to share homemade dessert recipes that make you drool from just one photo.

I WISH I HAD A DREAMY ORIGIN STORY BUT THE THING IS…

I grew up on box mixes and shortcuts. Being from the Midwest, we are all about using mixes to create super easy dessert recipes. And there’s nothing wrong with that! But there’s just something about the process of baking a dessert from complete scratch that makes the end product so much more rewarding.

Although, my mom kept an ARCHIVE of Taste of Home magazines which she occasionally snipped some recipes from. I remember she clipped a recipe for homemade brownie dessert pizza with peanut butter frosting and it completely opened my eyes to from-scratch baking. Literally all it took was a dessert pizza! From then on out, I had dreams of opening my own bakery and churned out cupcakes and cookies like nobody’s business.

I also have an insatiable sweet tooth which is why I love baking so much. Pulling together interesting flavors and techniques to create a beautiful dessert is what I live for.

BUT THEN I HAD TO CHANGE HOW I BAKED

Up until a few months ago, I was baking with nothing but your standard flour, sugar, butter, and all things dairy. While I still LOVE to bake this way and will continue to do so, I recently discovered that I have a sensitivity to both gluten and cow’s milk. Goodbye flour and butter! Ok well…not completely.

While I can have a few bites here and there, I can’t gorge on chocolate chip cookies like I used to. I break out, get so fatigued, lose focus, and develop an annoying headache. But it’s also pushed me to develop recipe modifications that not only help me, but are also useful to all of you!

I’m now discovering that so many others are having to cut gluten and/or dairy out of their lives. It’s still in the works, but you’ll definitely see more gluten and dairy free recipes on Butternut Bakery. It won’t be my entire blog, but I’m excited to add these into the mix to cater to more people that struggle with these dietary restrictions like I do.

I HOPE TO MAKE BUTTERNUT BAKERY A DESTINATION FOR ALL THINGS DESSERTS

When you land on my site, I want you to be able to find the BEST recipes for exactly what you’re looking for. From the perfect chewiness of a cookie, to the moist crumb of a cake, no matter if it’s gluten free, dairy free, or your standard recipe with the works. We’re all just here to get our sweet tooth fix!

ABOUT JENNA OF BUTTERNUT BAKERY

Jenna is originally from a very small town in Illinois where she grew up in her family home on Butternut Street. After graduating from college in Chicago, she and her fiance, Nick, moved to the sunny state of Arizona and could not be happier. They’ve also adopted an adorable pup named Lady, who you can often see all over the Butternut Bakery Instagram stories. And finally, after 2 years of running Butternut Bakery alongside an 8 to 5 job, the blog has now become her full time business.
